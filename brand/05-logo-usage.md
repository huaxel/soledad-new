# 05. Logo Usage Guidelines

## Primary Logo

The Hacienda La Soledad wordmark should be used as the primary identifier.

## Variations

| Version | Usage | File |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary (Dark)** | On white/light backgrounds | `logo-dark.svg` |
| **Inverted (Light)** | On black/dark backgrounds | `logo-light.svg` |
| **Monochrome (Black)** | Print, fax, low-res | `logo-mono.svg` |

## Clear Space

The logo must have a minimum clear space around it equal to the height of the "H" in "Hacienda". No other elements should intrude into this zone.

## Minimum Size

* **Digital**: Width no smaller than 120px.
* **Print**: Width no smaller than 30mm.

## What NOT To Do

* ❌ Do not stretch or distort the logo.
* ❌ Do not change the logo colors (e.g., pink, blue).
* ❌ Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds without a solid container.
* ❌ Do not add drop shadows, glows, or 3D effects.
* ❌ Do not rotate the logo.

## Favicon & App Icon

Use the "Espadaña" (bell tower) silhouette as the simplified icon for favicons and social media profile pictures. Color: Hacienda Gold on Sevilla Black.
